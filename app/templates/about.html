About Me

Hi, I’m Laura, a Computer Science student with a keen interest in technology, systems, and solving practical problems.

Over the past few years, I’ve built a foundation in IT, networking, and security through completing the CompTIA A+, Network+, and CCNA. Alongside my degree, I’ve been exploring how to apply what I’ve learnt by working on personal projects.

This website is one of those projects. Built with Flask, it’s a space for me to document my progress, experiment with new tools and technologies, and improve my coding skills. Recently, I’ve been particularly focused on Linux administration, virtualisation, and setting up a home lab using Proxmox for self-hosted solutions.

My main areas of interest include networking, cybersecurity, DevOps, and cloud computing. I enjoy exploring how these fields intersect and finding practical ways to apply them.
